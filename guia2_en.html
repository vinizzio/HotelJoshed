TRADITIONS, CULTURE AND FOOD

Typical Food.

Chugchucaras

This typical dish consists of sweet empanadas, pork, potatoes, fried banana, popcorn, toasted corn, and a piece of boiled pig skin. This is served with pork rhine and mote

Allullas and Cheese in a leaf

These are made with wheat flour and cooked in lard and egg. Cheese in a leat is served in achira leaf and wrapped in it which gives it a unique flavor. It is usually eaten with hot coffee.

PHOTO: DIEGO PAREDES B.

Corn Tortillas

These are also called " tortillas de palo" the main ingredient being corn flour cooked in lard, salt and other condiments. Before being made, cheese and fried onion is placed in the center. A better flavor is gotten by cooking them over an open fire until toasted.
